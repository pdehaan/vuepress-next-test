## Ingredients

- **&frac12; cup** oatmeal
- **&frac14; cup** quinoa
- **1 tsp** chia seeds
- **1 tsp** help seeds
- **1 tsp** something else
- **&frac14; cup** raisins
- **18 oz** almond milk

## Directions

1. Add everything to Instant Pot.
1. Cook on high pressure for 4 minutes, natural release.
1. Eat.
